Shrimp Scampi with Pasta

Shrimp scampi with pasta is the ultimate seafood pasta dish. Works with any pasta; angel hair is less filling.

Ingredients

  • 8 - 10 large shrimp
  • 16-ounce linguine
  • 2 table spoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 table spoon unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves shallots and garlic
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • 1/2 lemon juice
  • 1/2 tea spoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 cup parsley

Directions

  1. Boil pasta in well-salted water and drain, keep water for shrimp
  2. Cook shrimp for 5-6 minutes until opaque pink and white
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup pasta water for mixing if dry
  4. Mix shrimp, linguine and enough pasta water
