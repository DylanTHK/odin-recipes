Delicious Egg Salad for Sanwiches

This is a wonderful-tasting egg salad sandwich that you will definitely devour. It's really good on rye.

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon prepared yellow mustard
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika

Directions

  1. Place eggs in saucepan and cover with cold water
  2. Bring water to a boil and immediately remove pan from heat
  3. Cover eggs in hot water for 10-12 minutes
  4. Remove from hot water, cool in cold water, peel and dice
  5. Place diced eggs in bowl and stir in mayonnaise, mustard and green onion
  6. Season with salt, pepper and paprika
  7. Stir and sesrve on your favourite bread or crackers
